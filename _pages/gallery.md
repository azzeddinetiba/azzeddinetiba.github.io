## PhD Thesis — Video Abstract

*Machine learning strategies for accelerating numerical simulations of fluid-structure interaction*

This video presents a brief illustrative abstract of the main contributions of my PhD thesis, defended in 2025 at [CNAM](https://www.cnam.fr/) (Paris). The thesis explores data-driven reduced order models and machine learning techniques to accelerate fluid-structure interaction simulations.
